**Ditis Core** is a proprietary data intelligence platform — purpose-built for treasure discovery. It fuses centuries of archival documents, environmental signals, and sensor feeds into a single ranked list of actionable expedition targets.

It is not a search engine. It is a **probabilistic intelligence engine** that continuously refines its confidence scores as new data flows in.

## Data Ingest

Archives from Lloyd's of London, the Spanish Casa de Contratación, the Dutch VOC records, and hundreds of other primary sources — OCR-processed, NLP-extracted, geocoded, and ingested automatically. Every archival document feeds the engine.

## Fusion Engine

Every record is decomposed into entities: ships, people, locations, dates, cargo. Entities are resolved across sources and stored in a knowledge graph. A ship mentioned in a 1715 manifest, a newspaper storm report, and a modern sonar scan all link to a single canonical target.

## AI Scoring

Each target receives a composite score (0–100) across six weighted factors: Historical Confidence, Value Estimate, Location Precision, Legal Feasibility, Recovery Ease, and Sensor Validation. Scores update dynamically as new data arrives.

## Decision Layer

A ranked target dashboard, expedition planner with Monte Carlo ROI simulation, stage-gate workflow, and investor portal — all in a single operational interface.
